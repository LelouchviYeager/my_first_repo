How to play

  • Use the arrow keys to move the snake.
  • Eat the red apples to grow longer and increase your score.
  • Avoid hitting the walls or the snake's own body.
  • The game is played on a 15 × 15 grid and moves at 1 cell per second.

Small security note: this demo does not include antivirus software. Run it locally in a secure environment.
